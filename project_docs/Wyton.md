# Wyton Developers - Vendor & Bidding Management

## Overview
Wyton Developers is a white-label vendor onboarding and construction bidding portal that helps real estate developers manage contract bids, payment terms, and vendor registrations.

## Main Features & Functionality
- **Bidding Management & Leveling**: Developers post projects; vendors submit bids. The dashboard compares bids side-by-side (bid leveling) for price, trade, and timeline.
- **Contract & e-Signatures**: Creation of binding contract commitments and signing via PDF integration.
- **Vendor Registration & Directories**: Onboarding workflow checking vendor trade qualifications, licenses, and insurance, with custom verification checks.
- **Project Action Plan**: Scheduling commitments, milestone payouts, and payment terms.

## Tech Stack & Integrated Tools
- **Frontend**: React, TypeScript, Radix UI, Tailwind CSS, Axios, PDF-lib.
- **Backend & Database**: Express, Node.js, MongoDB.
- **Core Integrations**: Secure document uploads, contract generation, responsive portal layouts.
